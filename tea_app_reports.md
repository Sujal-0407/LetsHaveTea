# Tea Management App - Reports & Summaries

## 1. Faculty View (Personal Summary)

Every faculty member should be able to see their own history without bothering the Leader. 

*   **Current Month View:**
    *   **Total Spent:** ₹___ (e.g., ₹450)
    *   **Total Cups:** ___ (e.g., 30 cups)
    *   **Days Present vs Absent:** (e.g., Present: 20 Days | Absent: 5 Days)
*   **Transaction Log:** A simple list showing deductions for tea and deposits made to the Leader. 

## 2. Leader View (Group Summary)

The Leader needs to see the macro-level data and export it if necessary.

*   **Current Month Overview:**
    *   **Total Fund Balance:** ₹___ (The total money currently held by the Leader)
    *   **Total Cups Consumed:** ___ (Adding up everyone's cups)
*   **Individual Faculty Breakdown:** A list showing each faculty member and their stats.
    *   *Example:* John Doe - Total Spent: ₹450 - Cups: 30 - Balance: ₹200.

## 3. End of Month Processing
*   **Data Export:** The Leader should have a 1-click button to "Export as CSV/Excel". This is highly useful if they need to print it out or share it in a WhatsApp group.
*   **Carry Over:** Balances automatically carry over to the next month. There is no need to 'reset' balances to zero unless someone leaves the group.
